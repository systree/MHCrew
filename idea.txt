## 🔗 **1. GHL Integration (Bi-directional)**

---

## 📥 **Incoming (GHL → System)**

### Methods:

* Webhooks (primary)
* API polling/fetch (fallback or enrichment)

---

### Events to Handle:

* Job created
* Job updated
* Job cancelled
* Crew assigned to job

---

### Requirements:

* Validate payloads
* Idempotent processing (avoid duplicates)
* Upsert jobs and assignments
* Trigger:

  * Notifications
  * Internal updates

---

## 📤 **Outgoing (System → GHL)**

---

### When to Send Data Back:

#### 1. Job Status Updates

* Each status change in PWA → push to GHL

---

#### 2. Job Completion

* Send:

  * Completion status
  * Total time
  * Notes (if any)

---

#### 3. Cancellation

* Send:

  * Cancel status
  * Reason

---

#### 4. Attachments / Photos

* Upload or send URLs back to GHL
* Associate with job/contact

---

### Requirements:

* Use GHL API securely
* Retry on failure
* Log all outbound syncs

# 📍 **2. GPS / Location Capabilities**

---

## 🎯 Purpose

* Provide **real-time or periodic location visibility**
* Improve:

  * Dispatch awareness
  * Customer updates
  * Job tracking

---

## 📱 From PWA (Crew Device)

---

### 📡 Location Tracking Modes

#### ✅ 1. Passive Tracking (Recommended MVP)

* Capture location:

  * On app open
  * On status change
  * At intervals (e.g., every 5–10 mins while active job)

---

#### 🚀 2. Active Tracking (Optional Advanced)

* Continuous tracking during:

  * `enroute`
  * `in_transit`

---

---

## 📦 Data to Capture

* Latitude
* Longitude
* Timestamp
* Job ID
* Crew user ID

---

---

## ⚙️ GPS Triggers

Capture location when:

* Job status changes
* Crew marks:

  * Enroute
  * Arrived
  * In transit
* Periodic background interval (if app active)

---

---

## 🗄️ Storage

Store in backend:

* `job_locations` (or equivalent)

---

## 🔗 Sync to GHL (Optional but recommended)

* Send key location updates:

  * Enroute location
  * Arrival location

---

---

## ⚠️ Constraints (IMPORTANT)

* Must request user permission
* Must handle:

  * Permission denied
  * Low accuracy
  * Offline mode

---

---

## 🔋 Performance Considerations

* Do NOT track continuously by default
* Use:

  * Event-based tracking (preferred)
* Minimize battery usage

---

---

## 🔐 Privacy

* Only track during active jobs
* No tracking outside job context

---

---
