MOVER HERO CREW APP — FEATURES & CAPABILITIES
==============================================

WHAT IT IS
----------
A mobile web app (PWA) for moving company field crews. It connects directly
to your GoHighLevel CRM so crews always have the right job info on their phone,
and managers can see what's happening in real time.

Works on any phone via browser — no app store required. Can be "installed"
to the home screen like a native app.


FOR CREW MEMBERS
----------------
• Job Dashboard — see all upcoming and past jobs in a clean daily view
• Job Details — pickup/dropoff addresses, scheduled time, customer info,
  item summary, and crew notes — all pulled live from GHL
• Job Status Updates — tap to move a job through: En Route → Arrived →
  In Progress → Completed (or Cancelled). Status syncs back to GHL instantly
• Timesheet — automatic clock-in when a job starts, clock-out when done.
  Breaks can be logged. Total time is tracked per job
• Photo Upload — attach job site photos directly to a job from the phone camera
• GPS Logging — location is logged at key job status changes
• Push Notifications — get notified when a new job is assigned to you
• Works Offline — status updates and location logs queue up when there's no
  signal and sync automatically when connection returns


FOR ADMINS / MANAGERS
----------------------
• Admin Dashboard — overview of all crew, jobs, and system settings
• Crew Management — view and manage crew accounts
• Job Overview — see all jobs across the location with status and crew info
• Invoice Creation — create and send invoices to customers directly from a
  job, powered by GHL's invoicing system
• Invoice Settings — configure business name, logo, tax settings (e.g. GST),
  and invoice number prefix
• Pipeline & Stage Mapping — map GHL pipeline stages to job statuses so
  the CRM stays in sync automatically
• Push Notification Settings — control which events trigger notifications
  (e.g. job assigned to crew, invoice created, status changes)
• Admin Notifications — get notified when crew update job statuses or create/
  send invoices
• Sync Tools — manually re-sync jobs, pipeline stages, location details,
  and custom field definitions from GHL


FOR CUSTOMERS (Inventory Tool — separate link)
----------------------------------------------
• Self-Service Inventory Wizard — customers receive a unique link to list
  what furniture and items they're moving, room by room
• 8 room categories: Bedrooms, Lounge, Dining, Kitchen, Office, Garage,
  Special Items, Boxes
• Review and edit before submitting — helps the company give an accurate quote
• Mobile-friendly, no login required


INTEGRATIONS
------------
• GoHighLevel (GHL) — two-way sync. Jobs come from GHL opportunities,
  status changes push back to GHL in real time
• SMS OTP login — crew log in with their phone number (no passwords, no app store)
• Secure PIN — after first login, crews use a 4-digit PIN for quick access
• Multi-company support — one platform, fully isolated per GHL sub-account
